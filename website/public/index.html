Get to Know Me

Asthanga Yoga Practitioner

I am from Tumkur, Karnataka, India.

Yoga was first introduced to me during my childhood by my uncle, who has since served as my mentor in this practice for five years. He is my initial yoga guru, and the knowledge imparted during those formative years has profoundly influenced my life. Since 2015, I have been dedicated to practicing vinyasa yoga, further deepening my understanding and appreciation of this discipline.
